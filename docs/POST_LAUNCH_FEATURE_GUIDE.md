## The 30-Second Version

MyProfitPulse is a financial clarity app for service-based business owners — coaches, consultants, agencies, trades, and similar operators who can read a P&L but don't want to live inside one.

You upload a spreadsheet or enter a few numbers. The app gives you a **Business Health Score** (0–100), a simple dashboard, plain-English insights, and **what-if calculators** so you can answer questions like *"Can I afford to hire?"* or *"What happens if I miss my revenue goal?"* — before you make the decision.

Think of it as a friendly financial co-pilot, not accounting software. A fractional CFO runs $2,500–$5,000/month. MyProfitPulse is built for owners who aren't there yet but still need real guidance.

---

## Who It's For

MyProfitPulse works best for:

- **Service-based business owners** who want clarity without jargon
- Owners who already track numbers somewhere (spreadsheet, bookkeeper, QuickBooks) but struggle to turn those numbers into decisions
- Entrepreneurs deciding whether to **hire, raise prices, take on debt, or tighten spending**
- Anyone who wants a **single number** that tells them how the business is really doing

It's **not** a replacement for your bookkeeper or CPA. It's the layer on top that helps you *use* your numbers.

## What You Can Do Today (Live Features)

### 1. Sign Up & Get Started

- Create an account with name, email, business name, and industry
- **7-day free trial** — no credit card required at signup
- After the trial, subscribe at **$59.99/month** or **$599.88/year** (~$50/month)
- Early launch subscribers who signed up during the launch window keep their discounted rate automatically

### 2. Business Health Assessment (First Step)

Every user starts here. You can:

- **Upload a spreadsheet** (CSV or Excel) — AI reads your financials and pulls out the key numbers
- **Enter numbers manually** — cash in the bank, monthly revenue, monthly expenses, and a few other basics
- Answer a short set of plain-English questions if you prefer

**What you get:** A **Business Health Score from 0 to 100**, a written summary of what's going well and what needs attention, and personalized recommendations. The score is transparent — users can see what goes into it (cash, margins, runway, and more).

### 3. Dashboard — Your Daily Home Base

Once data is in, the dashboard shows:

- **Business Health Score** with a visual gauge (green / amber / red)
- **Cash position** — how much money is in the bank right now
- **Runway** — how many months the business can keep running at current spending
- **Profit and revenue trends**
- **AI insight** — a plain-English read on what's happening in the numbers

Traffic-light colors appear only on health signals — green means you're in good shape, amber means pay attention, red means act now.

### 4. Scenario Calculators — The Hero Feature

This is what makes MyProfitPulse different from a static dashboard. Four interactive calculators help owners test big decisions *before* they commit:

| Calculator | What it answers |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Break-Even** | "How much do I need to sell each month just to cover my costs?" |
| **Goal Planning** | "What monthly revenue do I need to hit my annual target?" |
| **Can I Hire?** | "Can I afford this salary without putting the business at risk?" |
| **Cash Runway & Shortfall Recovery** | "How long will my cash last?" and "If I miss my target this month, what do I need to do to catch up?" |

Each calculator gives **instant results plus an AI explanation in plain English** — not just a number on a screen.

During the free trial, Break-Even and Goal Planning are fully available. The other two unlock with a paid subscription.

### 5. Financial Reports

Three standard reports, built from the user's data:

- **Profit & Loss (P&L)** — revenue, expenses, and what's left over
- **Cash Flow** — money coming in vs. going out
- **Balance Sheet** — what the business owns, owes, and is worth

Charts and tables are designed for owners, not accountants. No dense jargon.

### 6. Data Entry & the Data Ledger

Users can keep their numbers fresh through:

- **Manual entry** — update cash, revenue, expenses, receivables, payables, and more by month
- **Spreadsheet upload** — drag and drop CSV or Excel; AI extracts the numbers and lets the user confirm before saving
- **Data Ledger** — a history of every saved snapshot, so users can see when data was updated and what the app is working from

The more current the data, the sharper the dashboard and scenarios become.

### 7. Pulse — Your In-App Guide

**Pulse** is the app's mascot — a friendly bear who pops up with helpful, plain-English tips as users move through the app. She introduces herself on first login and offers context on each major page (dashboard, reports, scenarios, data).

Users can **minimize Pulse** to a small icon if they want her out of the way. She stays minimized until they bring her back.

### 8. Glossary

A built-in glossary explains financial terms in everyday language — Revenue, Runway, Gross Profit, ROE, ROI, Break-Even, and more. Each term includes a simple definition, a real-world example, and a link to where it shows up in the app.

Great for owners who feel embarrassed asking "what does that mean?"

### 9. Settings & Account Management

Users can manage:

- **Profile** — name, email, avatar
- **Business info** — business name, industry
- **Notifications** — weekly summary emails and product update emails (on/off)
- **Billing** — view plan, switch monthly/annual, update card, cancel subscription, see payment history
- **Account** — change password, change email, delete account

### 10. Email Notifications

- **Weekly Summary** — a Monday-morning recap of business health and key metrics (users can turn this off)
- **Product Updates** — news about new features (optional)
- Auth emails (verification, password reset) are handled automatically

### 11. Security & Trust

- Secure login with email verification and password reset (6-digit code flow)
- Payments processed through **Authorize.net** — card numbers never touch our servers
- Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and QuickBooks-ready EULA published on the site

## Common Questions & How to Answer Them

**"Is this accounting software?"**  
No. It sits on top of whatever you already use — QuickBooks, a spreadsheet, or numbers from your bookkeeper. It translates your data into clarity and decisions.

**"Do I need QuickBooks?"**  
No. You can upload a spreadsheet or type in numbers manually. QuickBooks connection is coming soon for people who want automatic sync.

**"How is this different from QuickBooks reports?"**  
QuickBooks tells you what happened. MyProfitPulse tells you what it *means* and lets you test what happens *next* — with a health score, plain-English insights, and what-if calculators.

**"Who built this?"**  
MyProfitPulse is a Fusion 4 Business product, built in partnership with Apps Built With AI. Joyce Hayward's decades of fractional CFO and advisory work shaped the product vision.

**"Can my bookkeeper use it?"**  
Today it's designed for the business owner. Advisor/accountant sharing is on the roadmap.

**"What if my numbers aren't perfect?"**  
That's normal. Pulse literally tells users not to worry about perfection — just get numbers in and the app gets smarter as you update.

**"Is my data safe?"**  
Yes. Each user only sees their own data. Payments are handled by Authorize.net. We don't store raw credit card numbers.

**"Can I try it before paying?"**  
Yes — 7-day free trial, no credit card at signup.
