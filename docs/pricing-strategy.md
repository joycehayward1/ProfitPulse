## Current ProfitPulse Feature Set

- Financial health score and assessment
- Dashboard (cash on hand, revenue, expenses, runway, profit margin)
- What-if scenario planning (break-even, hiring, goal planning, runway)
- QuickBooks integration
- Spreadsheet/CSV upload for data entry
- Weekly email summaries
- Notification preferences

---

## Recommendation: Launch at $59/mo

The current feature set aligns with the $39-$59 range where most SMB financial tools cluster. Launching at **$59/mo** provides:

- **Low friction** for service-based business owners who haven't used a tool like this before
- **Competitive positioning** against Fathom, Float, Digits, and Pulse
- **Room to grow** — easier to raise prices or introduce a tier than to lower a price that isn't converting

### Suggested Launch Plan

**Essentials — $59/mo** (or $49/mo billed annually)

- Full dashboard + health score
- All scenario planning tools
- spreadsheet upload 
- Weekly email summary
- Email support

### Future Premium Tier — $99/mo (when ready)

The premium tier should be built around features that deliver **ongoing, personalized financial guidance** — not just tools, but outcomes. Potential differentiators:

- **Proactive smart alerts** — not just "here are your numbers" but "your runway dropped below 3 months, here's what changed and 2 actions to take." Pattern detection, anomaly flagging, trend warnings.
- **Custom KPI tracking** — let users define and track the metrics that matter to their specific business (e.g., revenue per employee, client acquisition cost, utilization rate)
- **Multi-entity / multi-business support** — many service business owners run more than one entity
- **Benchmarking** — "your profit margin is in the top 30% of service businesses your size" (requires aggregate data over time)
- **Advisor/accountant sharing** — read-only dashboard link for their accountant or bookkeeper
- **Priority support + onboarding call**

### Pricing Psychology

- Position against the fractional CFO ($1,500+/mo), not against other SaaS tools
- "The financial clarity of a CFO for $59/mo" is a compelling pitch for service business owners
- Annual discount (e.g., $49/mo billed yearly) locks in early users and reduces churn
