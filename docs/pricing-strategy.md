**The real alternative:** Most service-based business owners aren't using any of these. They're either flying blind, using spreadsheets, or paying $1,500-$3,000/mo for a fractional CFO.

## Launch Pricing (Decided)

**Single plan, two billing options:**

- **Monthly:** $59/mo
- **Annual:** $49/mo billed annually ($588/year) — 17% savings
- **7-day free trial** included

### What's Included

- Financial health score and assessment
- Dashboard (cash on hand, revenue, expenses, runway, profit margin)
- All 4 scenario calculators (break-even, hiring, goal planning, runway)
- QuickBooks integration
- Spreadsheet/CSV upload for data entry
- Weekly email summaries with smart alerts
- Email support

### Future Premium Tier (TBD)

When ready, a higher tier could be introduced around features that deliver **ongoing, personalized financial guidance** — not just tools, but outcomes. Potential differentiators:

- **Custom KPI tracking** — let users define and track metrics specific to their business (e.g., revenue per employee, client acquisition cost, utilization rate)
- **Multi-entity / multi-business support** — many service business owners run more than one entity
- **Benchmarking** — "your profit margin is in the top 30% of service businesses your size" (requires aggregate data over time)
- **Advisor/accountant sharing** — read-only dashboard link for their accountant or bookkeeper
- **Priority support + onboarding call**

Note: AI chat is not a tier differentiator — it's becoming table stakes.

### Pricing Psychology

- Position against the fractional CFO ($1,500+/mo), not against other SaaS tools
- "The financial clarity of a CFO for $59/mo" is a compelling pitch for service business owners
- Annual discount locks in early users and reduces churn
